Project Title: Digital Transformation Initiative

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
The organization seeks to implement a comprehensive digital transformation initiative to modernize our IT infrastructure and improve operational efficiency.

2. SCOPE OF WORK
- Develop and deploy a cloud-based enterprise resource planning (ERP) system
- Implement cybersecurity measures including firewalls, encryption, and access controls
- Migrate existing data to the new cloud platform
- Provide staff training on new systems
- Offer 24/7 technical support for 12 months post-implementation

3. TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS
- Minimum 5 years experience in ERP system development
- Proven expertise in cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
- ISO 27001 certification for information security
- Experience with data migration from legacy systems
- Knowledge of Agile development methodologies

4. BUDGET CONSTRAINTS
The total budget for this project is $750,000 USD.

5. TIMELINE
Project completion is required within 18 months from contract signing.

6. EVALUATION CRITERIA
Proposals will be evaluated based on:
- Technical approach and methodology (40%)
- Team qualifications and experience (30%)
- Cost-effectiveness (20%)
- Project timeline and milestones (10%)
